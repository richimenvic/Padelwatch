PadelScoreGT5

Huawei Watch app

A simple standalone padel scorekeeper for Huawei Watch. It helps users track points, games, sets, tie-breaks, and super tie-breaks directly from the watch.

PadelScoreGT5 does not require login, account registration, payments, network access, or personal data collection.
