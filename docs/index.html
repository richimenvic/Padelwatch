PadelScoreGT5

Simple padel scorekeeper for Huawei Watch

PadelScoreGT5 is a simple standalone padel scorekeeper designed for Huawei Watch. It helps users track points, games, sets, tie-breaks, and super tie-breaks directly from the watch.

The app does not require login, account registration, payments, network access, or personal data collection.
